Why does the amount I receive increase?

The NHS pension scheme has changed over time

Scheme rule changes in 1995, 2008 and 2015, mean you can receive money from your NHS Pension at different ages. You can still retire at 60 but won't receive money from the other schemes until later.

2008 2008 - 2015 Age 65
