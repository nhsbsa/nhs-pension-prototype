Why does the amount I receive increase?

The NHS pension scheme has changed over time

We call the age that you can take your pension without facing a reduction for early payment your 'normal pension age'.

This age changed with the scheme rule changes in 1995, 2008 and 2015.

Pensions scheme changes
Pension scheme Date paid into Normal pension age
1995 1990 - 2008 Age 60
2008 2008 - 2015 Age 65
2015 2015 - present Age 68
