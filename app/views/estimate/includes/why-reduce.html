Why is the amount I could get reduced?

The NHS pension scheme has changed over time

Scheme rule changes in 1995, 2008 and 2015, mean you can receive money from your NHS Pension at different times.

We call this the 'normal pension age'. This is the age that you retire from working for the NHS and have your pension paid without facing a reduction for early payment.

Pensions scheme changes
Pension scheme Date paid into Normal pension age
1995 1990 - 2008 Age 60
2008 2008 - 2015 Age 65
2015 2015 - present Age 68
